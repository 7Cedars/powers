# Powers 101 Organisation Specification

## Organisational Structure & Context

### *Vision & Mission*

Powers2Nouns is is an experiment to add specific powers to types of Nouns. It is not officially related to Nouns DAO, does not have any power over its decisions and does not control any of its assets. 

Powers2Nouns is funded separately from nouns DAO. Specific treats of Nouns DAOs are given articular powers to manage the Powers2Nouns treasury. In theory, this should result in changing value of nouns from the original Nouns DAO.
